    Discover a List of Events

    Campus Discovery Webpage

    Description

    This webpage application is designed to streamline the management and registration of campus events. Users can effortlessly register for upcoming events, access detailed information, and track their participation history.

    Problem it solves

    This application addresses the challenge of effectively organizing and disseminating information about campus events, eliminating the confusion and inefficiency often associated with manual event management and registration processes. By centralizing event listings, registration, and management into a single, user-friendly platform, it significantly improves accessibility for participants.
